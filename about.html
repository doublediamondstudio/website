About us

Hey there!

Double Diamond is a design studio based in Amsterdam and Paris.
The journey of our designers began more than 20 years ago, driven by a passion for digital innovation and excellent user experiences..

Our team has worked in all sorts of fields, like e-commerce, finance, consultancy, aviation, accounting, telecommunications, media, non profit, and culture.
From B2C to B2B, we excel in crafting seamless user experiences for small entrepreneurs and companies, as well as complex SaaS and backoffice solutions.

What is a Double Diamond?

The Double Diamond, is a design thinking framework that we use to solve problems step by step:

Discover: We learn everything about the problem.
Define: We figure out exactly what needs fixing.
Develop: We come up with lots of ideas.
Deliver: We make the best idea real and working.

This process makes sure we find the best solutions that work for your customers. It's our recipe for solving the right problems in the right way!
